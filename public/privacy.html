Privacy Policy

Thank You Jesus — Spiritual Companion App
Last updated: May 2025

Thank You Jesus (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

The App stores your preferences (language, denomination, message count) locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

When you send a message in the App, it is forwarded to the Anthropic API to generate a response. Anthropic may process this data according to their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy. We do not store conversation history on our servers.

2. Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not collect or store your payment card details. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe in accordance with their privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Information

  • To generate AI-powered spiritual responses to your questions
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To remember your language and denomination preferences (stored locally only)

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic — AI response generation (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — App hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — Typography (Privacy Policy)

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and contains only religious and educational content.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Data stored locally on your device can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app storage.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete from our servers. For data processed by Anthropic or Stripe, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at thankyoujesus.app/privacy.
